Frequently Asked Questions

Net Metering is a localized regulatory framework mechanism allowing homeowners and business entities to send excess power back to regional electricity distribution utilities (such as LESCO, K-Electric, IESCO, etc.). This securely offsets your total monthly utility billing consumption.
Most consumers experience an 85% to 90% reduction in monthly electricity bills. Exact savings vary depending on array size, panel configuration angles, roof exposure to sunlight, and consumption habits.
Physical system installation on residential rooftops usually takes 3 to 5 business days. Securing formal net metering approval and meter swaps from local power companies normally requires an additional 4 to 6 weeks.
Tier-1 international brands listed on Bloomberg—such as Longi Solar, Jinko Solar, JA Solar, and Canadian Solar—consistently deliver reliable efficiency. We recommend specific models based on your property layout and budget.
Premium panels include a 12 to 15-year factory material warranty along with a 25 to 30-year power production guarantee. Central grid-tied inverters carry standard structural replacement protection for 5 full years.
